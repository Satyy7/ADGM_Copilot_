    Gaming and Gambling Activities within ADGM

    The Registration Authority of the Abu Dhabi Global Market (“ADGM”) does not issue licences for, or register entities undertaking, gaming or gambling activities.

    Restricted Activities

    Restricted gaming and gambling activities within ADGM include, without limitation, any activity that:

    • involves the placing, acceptance, facilitation, or settlement of a bet, wager, or stake, where a person risks or stakes money or value on an outcome determined wholly or predominantly by chance, randomness, or an uncertain future event over which the participant has no material influence; or
    • involves participation in, or facilitation of, casino-style games, gaming systems, or prize-based schemes, where a person risks or stakes money or value and money or value may be won, transferred, or redeemed, and the participant has no material influence; and
    • is structured, promoted, or operated in a manner that is economically or functionally equivalent to gaming, gambling, or betting, regardless of the terminology used or the form of consideration involved.

    The Registrar will also refuse to licence holding activity or any other passive licensed activity for a subsidiary undertaking that undertakes gaming or gambling activity.

    Clarification on Non‑Gambling Activities

    For the avoidance of doubt, gaming or gambling activity, within the ADGM, does not include games or machines where:

    (i) the outcome is predominantly determined by the skill, dexterity, knowledge, or reaction time of the participant; and
    (ii) the game or machine is arcade‑style in nature and is not economically or functionally equivalent to gambling.
